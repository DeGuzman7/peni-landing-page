About Us

PENI is a mobile application that seeks to help you achieve your financial goals, improve your income management culture by helping you setup a budget, track your spending, manage your expenses and also get advice from personal financial experts.

BUDGET

Accountability starts with proper planning. Section your income to cover needs, wants, emergencies and savings in just a few easy steps.

TRACK

TRACK Keep a record of all debit transactions as they occur. Get a detailed report per time of how you’ve spent your money based on your budget and recorded expenses. Receive notifications when you approach your budget limits.

FINANCIAL EXPERT

Get connected to all the financial assistance you need in just one click. Our experts are always available to help you get back on track with your financial goals.
